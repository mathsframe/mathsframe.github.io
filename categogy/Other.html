About Other games

You'll have to battle up against the strange MathsFrame, a figure that went viral and looks like a human head stuck in a toilet with Other games. Your job is clear: drive your car and fight back against these toilet-shaped monsters so they don't take over the world.

Get the right tools and trucks to fight the toilet invasion, but watch out for bigger MathsFrames that can eat you. Get coins to buy upgrades like skins and new cars, and get stars to get into new game types and levels.

How to play Other games:

  • To move your car, press WASD or the button keys.
  • You can attack MathsFrames by tapping on them or driving your car into them.
  • To speed up, press the "boost" button.

Get stars and coins to unlock new material and make your character better. The MathsFrames want to do bad things. Can you stop them and save the world from this strange threat? In Cars vs. MathsFrame, you can find out!
